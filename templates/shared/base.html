Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ticketless.io (“we”, “our”, “the Service”) collects, stores, and uses personal information from event organizers and attendees. We provide a fully managed event management and ticketing solution, allowing organizers to focus on hosting great events while we handle ticketing, payments, bookings, access validation, and attendee support.

Which entities does this privacy policy cover?

This policy applies to Ticketless.io, our platform services, and any tools or integrations used to process event data and attendee information. It covers both event organizers who use our service and attendees who purchase tickets through Ticketless-supported events.

What is personal information?

Personal information refers to any data that identifies an individual, including names, contact details, payment information, event attendance data, or device identifiers collected when interacting with our platform.

What personal information do we collect?

We collect information necessary to operate event ticketing and payments, including:

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Event registration and attendance details
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely via external gateways)
  • Device, IP, and usage analytics to improve the platform
  • Support requests and communication history
Why do we collect your personal information?

We collect personal data to:

  • Process ticket purchases and registrations
  • Validate access at event entry
  • Support organizers in managing attendees
  • Provide customer support and fraud prevention
  • Maintain compliance with financial and legal regulations
When and how do we collect your personal information?

Information is collected when:

  • You sign up as an event organizer
  • You purchase or register for a ticketed event powered by Ticketless.io
  • You interact with our platform, customer support, or verification tools
  • Event organizers upload attendee lists or submit event details
What if you don’t want us to collect your personal information?

Certain information is required for processing tickets and granting event access. Opting out may result in limited or denied access to events or platform services. You may request deletion of your data after the event unless regulatory retention applies.

What if you don’t want to receive further communications?

You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time. However, transactional communications (e.g., tickets, payment confirmations, event updates) cannot be disabled as they are required to fulfill your purchase.

When do we disclose your personal information?

We only share data with:

  • Event organizers for events you register for
  • Payment providers to process transactions
  • Verification vendors for fraud prevention and access control

We do not sell attendee data to third parties.

How can I access, correct, update or delete my data?

You may request a copy of your personal data or request deletion by contacting us. Event organizers may access attendee data only for events they manage. Some data may be retained for compliance with financial or legal regulations.

How do we store and protect your information?

We store data in encrypted systems and restrict access to authorized personnel only. Financial data is processed by PCI-compliant payment processors; we do not store raw card details on our servers.

If you have questions about privacy, data protection, or our security policies, contact us:
